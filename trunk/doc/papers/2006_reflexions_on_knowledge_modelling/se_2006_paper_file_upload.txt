Abstract:

This article reports about an ongoing research investigating the
possibilities for applying inter-disciplinary concepts to software
system design. The new resulting programming philosophy is based on
firstly, a distinction of statics and dynamics, secondly a knowledge
 schema structuring models and their meta information
hierarchically, and thirdly the separation of state- and logic
knowledge. It solves many of the problems existing in classical
programming paradigms and languages and may have the potential to
replace these in the long run.
